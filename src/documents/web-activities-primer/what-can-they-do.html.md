What can I do with web activities?
==================================

Lots of things. Here's an initial list:

* configure
* costcontrol/balance
* costcontrol/data_usage
* costcontrol/telephony
* dial
* new (e.g. type: “websms/sms”, “webcontacts/contact”)
* open
* pick (e.g. type: “image/png”)
* record
* save-bookmark
* share
* test
* view
